<p><p>The solar roof tiles market primarily features two types of solar cells: monocrystalline and thin-film. Monocrystalline solar cells are made from high-purity silicon and known for their high efficiency and space-saving design, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, thin-film solar cells are lighter and more flexible, allowing for a variety of installations but typically offer lower efficiency compared to their monocrystalline counterparts. Each type caters to different consumer needs based on space, budget, and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
