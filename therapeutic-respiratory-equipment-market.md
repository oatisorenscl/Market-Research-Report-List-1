<p><ul><li>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Devices</li><li>Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The therapeutic respiratory equipment market includes devices like Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) and Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) devices. CPAP devices deliver a steady stream of air to maintain open airways, primarily used for treating obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, BiPAP devices provide two pressure levels: a higher pressure for inhalation and a lower pressure for exhalation, making them suitable for patients with complex respiratory issues. Both types enhance respiratory support and improve patient quality of life.</p></p>
