<p><p>The CNC machine vise market includes single station, dual station, and multiple station types, each catering to different manufacturing needs. Single station vises are designed for simplicity and individual workpieces, ideal for basic operations. Dual station vises enable simultaneous processing of two parts, improving efficiency and productivity. Multiple station vises, on the other hand, can hold several workpieces at once, maximizing throughput and minimizing setup times, making them suitable for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
