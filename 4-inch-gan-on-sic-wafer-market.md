<p><p>The 4-inch GaN on SiC wafer market can be categorized into two main types based on conductivity: conductive and semi-insulated. Conductive wafers are designed for applications requiring high electron mobility and low resistance, making them ideal for power electronics and RF devices. Semi-insulated wafers, on the other hand, provide controlled conductivity, which minimizes unwanted capacitance and enhances isolation in high-frequency applications. Both types are essential in advancing semiconductor technology and enabling efficient energy solutions across various industries.</p></p>
