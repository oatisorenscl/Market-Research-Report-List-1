<p><strong>The Dairy Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermally Reversible Gel</li><li>Thermo-Irreversible Gel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dairy stabilizers market is segmented into thermally reversible gels and thermo-irreversible gels. Thermally reversible gels can melt and re-form when subjected to temperature changes, allowing for flexibility in texture and mouthfeel in products like yogurts and desserts. In contrast, thermo-irreversible gels maintain their structure once set, providing stability and consistency in dairy items such as cheeses and puddings. Both types enhance product quality, shelf-life, and sensory attributes, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the dairy sector.</p></p>
