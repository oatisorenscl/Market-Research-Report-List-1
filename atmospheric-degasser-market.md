<p><strong>The Atmospheric Degasser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Atmospheric Degasser market can be categorized into two types: vertical and horizontal systems. Vertical degassers are designed to process fluids in an upward flow, enhancing gas removal efficiency through increased surface area exposure to atmospheric pressure. In contrast, horizontal degassers operate with a side-to-side flow, allowing for larger treatment volumes and easier maintenance access. Both types serve to eliminate dissolved gases from liquids, improving the quality and performance of fluids in various industrial applications, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemicals.</p></p>
