<p><strong>The Medical Scavenging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Vacuum Systems</li><li>Passive Vacuum Systems</li><li>Centralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical scavenging system market encompasses various types: </p><p>1. **Active Vacuum Systems** use powered suction to efficiently collect and remove anesthetic gases during surgical procedures, ensuring a safer environment for patients and staff.</p><p>2. **Passive Vacuum Systems** rely on natural airflow and gravity to remove waste gases, offering a simpler and cost-effective alternative without mechanical components.</p><p>3. **Centralized Systems** involve a network of scavenging units connected to a main pipeline, providing a robust solution for large medical facilities needing efficient gas management across multiple operating rooms.</p></p>
