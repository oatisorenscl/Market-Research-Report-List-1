<p><p>The Dry Eye Diagnosis Systems market includes several specialized technologies for assessing ocular surface conditions. Anterior Segment OCT (AS-OCT) provides detailed imaging of the eye's anterior structures. Ocular Surface Thermographers measure temperature variations to assess inflammation. Corneal Topographers map the cornea’s curvature, while Osmolarity Testing Devices quantify tear film stability. Fluorophotometers measure tear dynamics, and Interferometers analyze lipid layer thickness. Meibography Devices visualize meibomian gland health. Other devices may include novel diagnostics or multifunctional systems that enhance dry eye assessments.</p></p>
