<p><p>The Endovascular Stent Graft market is categorized into three main types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. Synthetic stent grafts are made from durable materials like polyester or polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), offering strength and resistance to degradation. Biological stent grafts utilize natural tissues, promoting integration and reducing rejection risks. Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic and biological materials, aiming to enhance biocompatibility while maintaining structural integrity. Each type serves specific clinical needs, addressing various vascular conditions through tailored approaches.</p></p>
