Conversely, petrolatum-based mastics provide effective moisture barriers and corrosion resistance, suitable for various industrial applications. Both types are essential in extending the lifespan of structures by preventing rust and deterioration, thereby ensuring optimal performance and reliability in diverse conditions.</p></p>
