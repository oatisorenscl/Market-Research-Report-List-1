<p><p>The wave energy devices market encompasses various technologies designed to harness ocean wave energy. Point absorbers capture energy from dynamic wave motion using buoy-like structures. Oscillating water columns utilize air pressure changes caused by wave action to generate electricity. Oscillating body converters involve floating structures that move with waves to produce energy. Overtopping wave power devices utilize incoming waves to create a reservoir, allowing for energy generation through turbines. Other devices include submerged pressure systems and hybrid technologies, expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
