<p><strong>The Curing Oven for Display Panel Production Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Air Heating</li><li>Infrared IR Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Curing ovens for display panel production utilize different heating methods to ensure optimal curing of materials. Hot air heating employs convection to circulate heated air, effectively curing large surface areas uniformly. This method is energy-efficient and suitable for various substrates. In contrast, infrared (IR) heating uses electromagnetic radiation to directly heat materials, allowing for faster curing times and energy savings. IR heating is particularly beneficial for delicate components, as it minimizes heat exposure to unaffected areas while providing targeted energy application.</p></p>
