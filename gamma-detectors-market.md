<p><ul><li>Scintillation Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor-based Detector</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gamma detectors are categorized into three main types: scintillation detectors, semiconductor-based detectors, and other types. Scintillation detectors use materials that emit light when gamma radiation interacts with them, making them highly sensitive and widely used in medical and industrial applications. Semiconductor-based detectors leverage semiconductor materials to directly convert gamma rays into electronic signals, offering excellent energy resolution. Other types encompass various detection technologies, such as gas-filled detectors and portable systems, catering to specific use cases in environmental monitoring and security.</p></p>
