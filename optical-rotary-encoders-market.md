<p><strong>The Optical Rotary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical rotary encoders market is primarily categorized into incremental and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders provide relative position changes, generating pulses with each rotation, making them suitable for applications requiring speed and direction feedback. In contrast, absolute encoders deliver unique position values at any given angle, ensuring precise and consistent readings even after power loss. Both types serve various industries, including robotics, automation, and automotive, contributing to efficient motion control and improved system performance.</p></p>
