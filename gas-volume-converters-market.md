<p><p>Gas volume converters (EVCs) are essential for accurately measuring gas volumes in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel EVCs and double-channel EVCs. Single-channel EVCs are designed to handle one gas stream at a time, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for smaller operations. In contrast, double-channel EVCs can simultaneously measure two gas streams, enhancing efficiency and data collection capabilities for larger or more complex systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
