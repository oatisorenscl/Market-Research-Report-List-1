<p><strong>The Hydraulic Hand Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump</li><li>Double Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic hand pump market comprises two main types: single acting and double acting hydraulic hand pumps. Single acting hydraulic hand pumps operate by using hydraulic fluid in one direction, providing power on the upstroke while returning fluid passively, making them simpler and often used for lighter applications. In contrast, double acting hydraulic hand pumps utilize hydraulic fluid on both the up and down strokes, allowing for greater efficiency and higher pressure output, making them suitable for more demanding tasks.</p></p>
