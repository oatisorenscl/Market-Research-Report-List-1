<p><p>The piezoelectric composite market is primarily segmented into two main types: piezoelectric ceramics and polymers. Piezoelectric ceramics are inorganic materials known for their high electrical sensitivity and mechanical stability, making them ideal for sensors and actuators. In contrast, piezoelectric polymers are flexible materials that offer better elasticity and lower density, suitable for applications requiring lightweight and conformable designs. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and medical devices, driving innovation and enhancing functionality.</p></p>
