<p><p>The Protein Binding Assay market is categorized into several types, including equilibrium dialysis, ultrafiltration, ultracentrifugation, and other methods. Equilibrium dialysis measures the distribution of a drug between a protein and a solution, ensuring accurate binding assessment. Ultrafiltration uses a membrane to separate free drug from protein-bound drug. Ultracentrifugation applies high-speed spinning to isolate bound versus free fractions based on density. Other methods may include liquid chromatography or mass spectrometry, catering to specific experimental needs and accuracy requirements.</p></p>
