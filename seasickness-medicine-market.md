<p><p>The seasickness medicine market is categorized into three main types: anticholinergics, antihistamines, and others. Anticholinergic medications, like scopolamine, work by blocking signals to the brain that trigger nausea. Antihistamines, such as dimenhydrinate, counteract the effects of histamine to reduce motion sickness symptoms. The "others" category includes alternative remedies and natural supplements, which may offer varying degrees of relief. Collectively, these options provide consumers with diverse choices to manage seasickness effectively while at sea.</p></p>
