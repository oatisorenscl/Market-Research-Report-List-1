<p><strong>The Smart Elevator Control Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Automatic Operation Panel</li><li>Selective Collective Operation Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart elevator control panel market comprises two primary types: Single Automatic Operation Panels and Selective Collective Operation Panels. Single Automatic Operation Panels allow users to input their desired floor, automatically routing the elevator without additional control, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, Selective Collective Operation Panels enable multiple simultaneous floor calls, optimizing efficiency by grouping stops based on demand, which is better suited for larger buildings. Both technologies enhance user convenience and improve elevator performance in varying contexts.</p></p>
