<p><p>Desalination technologies encompass various methods for converting seawater into potable water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) utilizes a semi-permeable membrane to separate salts and impurities from water. Multi-stage Filtration (MSF) employs a series of filters to remove contaminants in multiple steps. Multi-effect Distillation (MED) involves evaporating water to separate salt, using heat recovery to enhance efficiency. Other methods may include electrodialysis and nanofiltration, contributing to a diverse market that addresses global freshwater scarcity through innovative solutions.</p></p>
