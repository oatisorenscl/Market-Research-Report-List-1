<p><p>The synthetic adhesives market consists of several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. Thermoplastic adhesives, known for their reversible bond formation, are ideal for applications requiring flexibility. Elastomer adhesives offer high elasticity, making them suitable for bonding dissimilar materials under varying temperatures. Thermoset adhesives cure to a permanent bond and provide excellent chemical and heat resistance, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Other categories may include pressure-sensitive and ultraviolet-curing adhesives, catering to specialized industrial needs. Together, these contribute to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
