<p><strong>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assay</li><li>Competitive Assay</li><li>Multiplex Detection Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatographic assay kit market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Sandwich Assay**: This method captures target analytes between two antibodies, enhancing sensitivity and specificity, commonly used for larger molecules like proteins. </p><p>2. **Competitive Assay**: This approach involves competing binding between the target analyte and a labeled antigen, typically used for smaller molecules, offering rapid results. </p><p>3. **Multiplex Detection Assay**: This innovative technique simultaneously detects multiple analytes in a single test, maximizing efficiency and valuable in complex diagnostics.</p></p>
