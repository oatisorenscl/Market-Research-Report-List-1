<p><strong>The Disposable Breathing Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Filter</li><li>Electrostatic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable breathing filters market consists of two primary types: mechanical filters and electrostatic filters. Mechanical filters trap particles through physical barriers, effectively capturing dust, pollen, and pathogens by using materials such as fibrous mats. In contrast, electrostatic filters utilize static electricity to attract and hold particles, providing an efficient means of filtration by enhancing particulate capture without a significant increase in airflow resistance. Both types cater to various applications, ensuring cleaner air in medical and industrial settings.</p></p>
