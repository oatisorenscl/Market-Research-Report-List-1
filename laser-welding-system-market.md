<p><p>The laser welding system market primarily comprises two main types: solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines utilize solid-state materials like crystals or diodes to generate laser light, offering high efficiency and precision for metal joining. In contrast, CO2 laser welding machines employ carbon dioxide gas to produce the laser beam, providing powerful output suitable for thicker materials. Each type serves distinct applications and industries based on factors like material compatibility and operational efficiency.</p></p>
