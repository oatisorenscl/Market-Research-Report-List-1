<p><p>The Red Cell Filter Market encompasses three primary types: whole blood transfusion, platelet transfusion, and red cell transfusion. Whole blood transfusion involves administering all components of blood, including red cells, plasma, and platelets, to patients. Platelet transfusion focuses on providing platelets to manage conditions like thrombocytopenia. Red cell transfusion specifically targets red blood cells to address anemia or significant blood loss. Each type requires specialized filters to ensure safety, efficacy, and removal of contaminants during the transfusion process.</p></p>
