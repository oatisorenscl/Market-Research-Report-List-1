<p><ul><li>Active Scan Code</li><li>Scanned by Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>QR Scan Payment market is divided into two main types: Active Scan Code and Scanned by Others. </p><p>In the Active Scan Code market, consumers initiate the payment by scanning a merchant's QR code using their mobile device. This method promotes direct interaction with the point of sale. </p><p>Conversely, the Scanned by Others market involves the user presenting their QR code, which is then scanned by the merchant's device. This approach streamlines transactions, enhancing convenience for both parties involved in the payment process.</p></p>
